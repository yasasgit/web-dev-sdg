Port City - Luxury Hotel Development

Project Name :

Port City - Luxury Hotel Development

Proposed Project Description :

Luxury branded hotel development. Plot 2-01-11 is for hotel use in the Island Living District with a site area of 24,324.16 Sq.m. The plot is located at the southeast corner of the Island Living District, overlooking the Marina District. The Island Living District is predominantly residential district with few mixed use developments and hotel properties as key anchor commercial developments. The hotel is located at the starting point of the beach and offers great panoramic views of the existing CBD, the financial district, the Marina district and the Indian Ocean. Given the prime location, the architecture of the building is conceived to be iconic. Majority of the building will be for hotel use, with portion for retail use mainly located within the podium development along ground levels.
