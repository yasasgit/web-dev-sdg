Boat Building And Repairing

Project Name :

Proposed Boat Building And Repairing Facility At Port Of Trincomalee

Proposed Project Description :

Government and the SLPA had identified the requirement of developing the Port of Trincomalee as a regional port for industrial activities. The several development projects have been implemented in Port to enhance the industrial activities. Further, the National Ports Master Plan and the Trincomalee District Master Plan of UDA have identified the Mudcove area for ship repair facility development.

NA

Expected method of partnership :

PPP

Employment generation :

After commencement of this facility will create direct and indirect employment specially for skill labors for the region

Market potential :

This has been identified in the National Port master Plan 2019

NA

2023

Approval of the cabinet required for nominating the PC and SCAPC to initiate procurement

Not estimated yet

Benefits to the Investor :
