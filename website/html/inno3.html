Genesis Ark 1 Bike



        You may recall that we recently introduced you to Rodha, Sri Lanka's first electric bike to be launched in Colombo. So in the last few days you may have seen information about another unique Sri Lankan product like this on social media So in recent days you may have seen information about another unique Sri Lankan product like this on social media. Genesis Core Technologies recently unveiled Genesis Ark 1, the first electric super bike in Sri Lanka, and is not only the first Electric Super Bike in Sri Lanka, but also the first Electric Super Bike in Southeast Asia.

                        It is also the first AI Powered Electric Bike in the world. With a power of 82KW, this super electric bike can reach speeds of up to 120kmph and the 14.4KW battery has a driving range of 180 miles or 260km. In addition, Genesis has added a vibration indicator to the handle for driver and pedestrian safety, as well as navigation, GPS and Android OS support by connecting your mobile phone to the 10-inch large screen mounted on this electric motorcycle. Shows.

        The keyless motorcycle is powered by a fingerprint and has a clutchless, direct drive transmission without a clutch. In addition, this super electric bike is equipped with a number of advanced technologies such as auto stand, voice enable, regenerative declaration, AC induction motor. Although no pricing or release date has been reported here, we all hope to see this motorcycle, which is hailed as Sri Lanka's first super electric bike, in the market in the near future.
