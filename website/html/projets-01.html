Dedduwa Lake Resort

Project Name :

Dedduwa Lake Resort at Galle District

Proposed Project Description :

It comprises of water bodies, marshy lands and abundant paddy lands. This project covering 1,800 ha of wetland lies to the east of the present Bentota Holiday Resort. The development plan consists of 5 to 7 Star hotels, water sport activities and a cultural village which is to be linked by the re-opening of old water ways to Madhu Ganga 20 km to the South and also developing the area surrounding the Dedduwa Lake
